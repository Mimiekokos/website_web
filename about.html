BACKGROUND ON MIMIE'S SHOES

The company, currently known as Mimie's Shoes is a shoe selling company that started operating in 2020
This company started operating in Chitungwiza after realizing that the residents of Chitungwiza go to town in order to purchase shoes
The business is florishing and this has led to more branches being opened all over the country
